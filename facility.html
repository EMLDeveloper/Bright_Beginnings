Operating Hours and Care Services

Our daycare operates Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 5:30 PM, offering a flexible schedule to accommodate your needs. During these hours, we provide a range of services designed to support your child's growth and well-being. Our care includes nutritious meals such as breakfast, lunch, and snacks, along with engaging activities like arts and crafts, outdoor play, and early learning sessions.

Meals

We provide healthy meals at our daycare, including a nutritious breakfast, a wholesome lunch, and tasty snacks.
